Fertility Salad

Rev. Shannon Walker, North Carolina

  • 4 lg. cucumbers (peeled and sliced)
  • 1/3 c. dry chamomile flowers
  • 1/3 c. fresh dill (chopped)
  • 1/2 c. small capers
  • 1/2 c. green onion
  • 1/4 c. grape seed oil
  • 1/4 c. balsamic vinegar

Mix together in a large bowl and garnish with sprigs of fresh mint,keep chilled.

This recipe has been served at several Hand Fastings over the years within my clan.I'm happy to say that three out four couples were blessed with child by years end.
