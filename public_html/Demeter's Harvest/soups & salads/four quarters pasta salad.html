Four Quarters Pasta Salad

Cindy Harris

  • 1 (12-oz) box of tricolor Rotini, cooked, rinsed in cold water, drained.
  • 1 med. cucumber, peeled and chopped (These two represent EARTH)
  • 1 small sweet onion, diced
  • 1 red pepper diced (this represents FIRE). May also add handful of grape tomatoes.)
  • 1 cup diced chicken or turkey (AIR)
  • 2 cups mixed shrimp, fake crab and/or fake lobster (WATER)
  • 1 (16 oz) bottle Poppyseed Salad dressing (Laura Lynn, the Ingles brand, works best, for those who live in the SE. Other brands are not as sweet, so you may wish to add 1 Tblsp. honey.)
  • 1-4 heaping Tblsp Miracle Whip or Mayo to taste

Mix pasta and salad dressing well, then add and mix other ingredients. Add Mayo to taste. Enjoy!
