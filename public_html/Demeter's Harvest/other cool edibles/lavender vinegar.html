Lavender Vinegar

Chi-Kali, Arizona

  • Distilled white vinegar
  • Lavender flowers

Flavored vinegars and stronger ones will compete with the herb for your senses.

Place a large handful of Lavender flowers in a pint and a half of vinegar. Let stand 4-6 weeks. Use it as a dressing for fruit salads .

You may use violets, or any other edible flowers as well.
