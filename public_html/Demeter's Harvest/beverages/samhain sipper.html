Samhain Sipper

Sapphire Angelica Brennan

  • 1 c. (8 oz) Martinellis apple juice
  • 1 tsp. each of the following:
  • dried and cut-up hibiscus flowers
  • rose hips
  • lemongrass
  • 1 slice fresh lemon
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Combine all the dried herbs and place them in a tea bag, or in a small muslin bag. Pour the apple juice into a microwave safe mug and microwave until steamy hot, about one minute on most microwaves, or heat juice on stove to a bare simmer. Add herbs and steep for 5-10 minutes, depending on how strong you wish it to be. Add the lemon slice and cinnamon stick.
