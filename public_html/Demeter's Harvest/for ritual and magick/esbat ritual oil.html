Esbat Ritual Oil

Misti Anslin Delaney

  • 1 oz. ritual oil base
  • 10 drops essential frankinscense oil
  • 10 drops essential myhrr oil
  • 15 drops essential santal oil
  • 10 drops essential attar of roses

Blend the oil base with the essential oils. Charge with energy focused for your intent. Use to anoint candles, add a few drops to ritual baths, add a drop or two to powdered incense. Before using it on skin, be sure that everyone has been tested for allergic reaction. Do Not ingest. Store in a cool dark place.
