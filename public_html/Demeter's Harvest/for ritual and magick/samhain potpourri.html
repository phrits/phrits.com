Samhain Potpourri

Heather Lynn, New Hampshire

  • 1/4 c. freshly fallen leaves (broken up)
  • 1 T. cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. cloves (sometimes I add a little bit more)
  • 2 T. dried pumpkin blossoms
  • A pinch of ginger
  • A few (depending on the size of your pot) baby pinecones

Simmer in a Cauldron during ritual or good-sized potpourri simmer pot for a festive fragrance to fill your home.
