Elder Oil

Rev. Shannon Walker, North Carolina

  • 10 crushed elder berries
  • 1 tsp. crushed frankincense
  • 1 tsp.crushed white sage
  • 3 drops patchouli oil
  • 1 c. grape seed oil

Combine all ingredients. Do not ingest.
