Peace And Justice Oil

Brianna Gloriana Skylark, Ohio

  • 1 oz. base oil (your choice — olive, sweet almond, jojoba — don't use a polyunsaturated oil!)
  • 1 oz. dropper bottle or other 1 oz. bottle
  • 3 drops chamomile oil
  • 3 drops rose oil
  • 5 drops ylang ylang oil
  • 5 drops lavender oil
  • 5 drops gardenia oil
  • 5 drops myrtle oil
  • 5 drops vervain oil
  • 5 drops lime oil
  • 5 drops rosemary oil
  • 13 drops black pepper oil

Add approximately half of the base oil to the bottle. Add each oil in turn, visualizing its energy entering and combining with the base oil and other oils. Roll the bottle between your hands to mix. When all oils are added, top off with remaining base oil (not too much!). Mix. When all ingredients are combined, charge for use according to your own methods. Use in spell work for justice and peace in your life or for the world, to stop personal or world violence by peaceful means, to catch criminals and other evildoers without harm to others and to bring those evildoers to justice. Do not ingest.
