Irish Rarebit

A modification of the Welsh classic

Carson Turner–Baedun Hill, North Carolina

  • 1 lb. of shredded Extra Sharp Irish Cheddar Cheese (substitute extra sharp New York Cheddar)
  • 1 T. of Butter (not margarine)
  • c. whole milk
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 T. of Bailey's Irish Crème
  • salt, pepper, chili to taste

Melt butter on medium high heat then add the cheese and salt, pepper, chili.

Simmer on low heat, stir to keep it moving.

As the cheese melts; gradually add the milk and Irish Crème, stirring constantly. Stir in the egg.

Simmer for about 2-3 minutes until it begins to thicken.

Serve hot over toast. Serves 4.
