Broccoli Casserole

Rhiannon, Tennessee

  • 1 lg. pkg of frozen broccoli
  • 1 can mushroom soup
  • 2 eggs (well beaten)
  • 1 c grated sharp cheese
  • 1/2 c mayonnaise
  • 2 T. chopped onion (finely chopped)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Cook broccoli as directed. Layer broccoli and pour oven mixture over it. Add cushion of Ritz crackers. Bake 30 minutes at 400°.
