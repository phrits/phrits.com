Goober Burgers

Darla Kaye Wynne, South Carolina

  • 1 lb. of lean hamburger meat
  • 4 Toasted hamburger buns
  • Mayo
  • Peanut Butter
  • Sliced Onion
  • Pickled Jalepenos
  • Sliced Tomato
  • 4 Slices of Mozzarella Cheese

Divide your hamburger meat into 4 patties. Press evenly and flat between hands. Fry or grill the hamburger meat until done. Melt cheese on top of hamburger. On one side of your hamburger bun, put mayonnaise and on the other, a thin layer of peanut butter. Put a slice of onion and tomato on top of your burger. Put about 6 slices of pickled jalepenos on each burger. Serve with chips or fries. Serves 4.
