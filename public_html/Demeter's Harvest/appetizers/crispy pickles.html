Crispy Pickles

Gramma Evelyn's Bread and Butter Pickles

Sherri Ellington, Georgia

  • 4 qt. thinly sliced medium cucumbers, unpared
  • 6 medium Vidalia (white) onions, sliced
  • 2 green peppers, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/3 c. coarse medium salt
  • 5 c. sugar
  • 3 c. cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tsp. tumeric
  • 1 1/2 tsp. celery seed
  • 2 tsp. mustard seed

Combine cucumbers, onions, pepper and garlic cloves; add salt, cover with cracked ice, mix thoroughly. Let stand 3 hours; drain well. Combine remaining ingredients; pour over cucumber mixture. Heat just to a boil; seal in hot, sterilized jars. Makes 8 pints.
