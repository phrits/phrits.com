Foaming Vanilla Honey Bath

  • 1 1/2-cup sweet almond oil (light olive or sesame oil may be substituted)
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup liquid soap (plain or flower scented)
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract

Place oil in mixing bowl stir in remaining ingredients until mixture is fully blended. Pour into a clean plastic bottle with a stopper or lid. Shake before using.
