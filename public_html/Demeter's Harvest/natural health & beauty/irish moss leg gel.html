Irish Moss Leg Gel

Candace

  • 1 tsp. Irish Moss
  • 1 c. Herbal infusion made with lemon, witch hazel and comfrey root powder.
  • 2 oz. Vodka 100 proof

Infuse the lemon, witch hazel and comfrey powder, let soak for a couple of days. Strain the liquid and add this the Irish Moss in a small pot. Simmer with a closed lid for 20 minutes or until the moss is soft and and jelly like. Strain through cheesecloth. Add the Vodka as a preservative. Mix together.

To use: Massage into the legs in upward motion. Very refreshing
