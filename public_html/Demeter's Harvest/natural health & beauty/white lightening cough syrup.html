White Lightening Cough Syrup

Rhiannon

Guaranteed to cut through nasty coughs and that nasty thick mucus that comes with them.

  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 Tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon honey
  • 2 Tablespoons warm water

Mix well and take 1 teaspoon as needed. The pepper may burn just a touch, but it's always ended my coughing fits, even the most severe, almost immediately.
