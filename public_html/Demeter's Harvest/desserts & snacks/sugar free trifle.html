Sugar Free Trifle

Lyra, Rhode Island

  • 1 sara Lee pound cake or store brand plain pound cake
  • 1 box frozen strawberries slightly thawed (no sugar added)
  • 1 bag frozen blueberries slightly thawed (no sugar added)
  • 1 lg. container fat free Kool whip

Cut the pound cake into slices then cut slices into strips,In a shallow baking dish layer cake, fruit,cool whip, cover with another layer cake and more fruit til you get 3 layers. Frost top with remaining Kool whip and refrigerate. It is a great diabetic dessert.
