Irish Tea Cakes

Chi Kali, Arizona

  • 1 c. butter, softened
  • 1 c. sugar
  • 1 c. powdered sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 c. vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 4 c. all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp. Nutmeg

Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and salt. Beat well. Beat in oil and vanilla. Sift dry ingredients together and mix in. Makes a very soft dough. Drop by scant teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet. Oil bottom of a glass, dip into a dish of sugar, flatten cookies on pan. (One oiling will do -–the oil in the cookies will take care of it). Dip in sugar before every cookie. Bake for 8 minutes at 350°.
