Spicy Apple Cake

Rayne Dancer

Combine:

  • 2 1/3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

set aside

Mix together:

  • 1/4 C unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 C sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Add:

  • 1 C buttermilk or sour milk
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

Mix well.

Stir in Flour mixture until just combined. Stir in:

  • 1/2 C raisins
  • 2 apples, pared, peeled and chopped.

Pour into sprayed 7x11" pan and bake at 350° F for 25-30 minutes.
