Strawberry Kiwi Snack Crackers

Darla Kaye Wynne, South Carolina

  • 4 oz. of Strawberry Yogurt
  • 3 Kiwis
  • Ritz Crackers
  • Fresh Mint leaves
  • Raspberries

Take a serving plate and decorate with mint leaves. Put the strawberry yogurt on your cracker and set decoratively on your serving plate. Peel and slice kiwi. Put one slice of kiwi on each cracker. Decorate with a raspberry on top of each kiwi slice. Chill in the refrigerator covered for one hour before serving.
