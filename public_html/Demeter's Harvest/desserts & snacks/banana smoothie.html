Banana Smoothie

Chi-Kali, Arizona

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 c. nonfat yogurt
  • 2 T. sugar
  • 1 c. crushed ice
  • 1 tsp. fresh lime juice
  • lime wedge and cherry for garnish

Combine the first 6 ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Pour the smoothie into a large glass and garnish with a lime wedge and cherry.
