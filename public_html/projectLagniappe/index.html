Project Lagniappe

Welcome! You've reached phrits's landing page for a project tentatively called Project Lagniappe. I'm writing to think, to learn and grow, and—I hope—to sway a few folks over to my way of thinking. My country is facing a dire crossroads, and I want to see us come through it with a more just and kind approach to damn near everything.

Approach

This will certainly evolve.

I'll list a few ideas—theses—I want to consider more deeply than I might for a Facebook comment, and I'll publish and link those as they come. I'll also add books and other readings as I think of them. I'll almost certainly allow myself a few side topics along the way.

With respect to pretty stuff like HTML formatting, that's another rabbit hole for me to avoid. I'll leverage what's easy to grab from my existing site, but I'm not going to worry too much about polish.

Everything is subject to change.

Who I Am

I'm not hard to find, identify, or dox. Other than my brilliant opinions, riotous dry wit, and unparalleled modesty, I'm you're average, old, married, white, American cis-guy. Mid 50s, college education, a few years in service, white collar. Bleeding heart liberal. Devout atheist. I've been online since 1992 or so, and some of my signal is easily found from my personal home page.
