Candace

In a saucepan mix:

  • 1/2 c. yellow mustard
  • 1 1/2 scoops coffee
  • 5 1/2 c. water

Heat to boiling and strain. place the water in a clean jar and add regular bond paper. keep paper in as you see fit Longer for darker, shorter for lighter (it does lighten as it dries) after you pull it out hang it on a clothesline outside and let it dry
