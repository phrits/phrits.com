Blond Brownies

Rayne Dancer

  • 2 2/3 C Flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2/3 C Butter
  • 1 pound light brown sugar, Box
  • 3 eggs
  • 6 oz chocolate chips
  • 6 oz butterscotch ships
  • 1 C Pecans

Combine flour baking powder and salt together and set aside. Melt butter and brown sugar in a saucepan; cool. When cool add eggs, one at a time, mixing well between each. Add flour mixture and mix well. Add chips and nuts. Spread in a jelly roll pan; bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes. Do not overcook. Batter is very thick. Cut in to squares when cooled.
