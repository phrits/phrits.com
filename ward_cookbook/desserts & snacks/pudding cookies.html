Pudding Cookies

Bethany Winterwinds, Minnesota

  • 1 1/2 c. Butter or Margarine
  • 1 c. Brown sugar
  • 1 c. Sugar
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 sm. pkg instant pudding (any flavor)
  • 2 tsp. Baking soda
  • 4 1/2 c. flour
  • Any kind of baking chips you want

Mix ingredients together and drop on cookie sheet. Bake at 350° for about 10 to 15 minuets.
