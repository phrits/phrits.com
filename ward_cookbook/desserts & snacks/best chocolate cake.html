Best Chocolate Cake

Rayne Dancer

  • 2 C. Flour
  • 2 C Sugar
  • 1/2 C Shortening
  • 3/4 C Water
  • 3/4 C Buttermilk
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 oz unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled.

Heat over to 350°F. Grease and flower 13x9x3" pan or 3 8" round pans or 2 9" pans. Beat all ingredients together in a large bowl on low speed for 30 seconds, scraping bowl constantly. Beat on high speed for 3 minutes, scraping bow constantly. Pour into pan(s). Bake for 40-45 minutes (rectangle pan) or 30–35 minutes (round pans), or until wooden pick comes out clean. Cool rounds 10 minutes; remove from pans. Cool completely and frost.
