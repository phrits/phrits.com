Bill's Baked Beans

Bill Haskins, New Hampshire

  • 1 lb. package of navy pea beans
  • 1 lg. onion diced
  • 1/2 lb. of salt pork
  • 3/4 c. dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 c. molasses

Put pea beans in a microwave safe bowl and cover with water. cook on high for 8 to 10 minutes.

Combine onion , salt pork , molasses and brown sugar in a crock pot.

Add beans and stir well.

Cook on low for 6 hours, switch to high and cook for another 3 hours.
