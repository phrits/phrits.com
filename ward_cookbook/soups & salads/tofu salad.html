Tofu Salad

Chi Kali, Arizona

  • 1 block firm tofu, cut in bite size
  • 1 lg. can salmon
  • 1 med. round vidalia onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, cut in cubes
  • green onion, cut fine
  • Chinese cabbage, cut fine

Sauce:

  • 3/4 c. shoyu
  • 1/2 c. vegetable oil
  • 1 T. sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp. mirin toasted sesame seeds

On platter arrange tofu, salmon, and vegetables in order given. Pour sauce just before serving.
