Par's Scampi

  • 2 pounds shrimp peeled and de-veined
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons chopped garlic
  • 2 tablespoons Old Bay Seasoning
  • 3 cups white wine
  • 1 cup shredded Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 large box or 2 small boxes angel hair pasta (cooked)

Place butter, garlic, oil and seasoning in a pot or pan and cook on low until butter is melted. Add in shrimp and cook until done. Pour in 2 cups wine and drink the other. Cook your pasta. Let shrimp simmer on low for 5 minutes or until the pasta is done%20%26%20you're done drinking your glass of wine.

Pour whole concoction over angel hair pasta. Add shredded romano and parmesan.
