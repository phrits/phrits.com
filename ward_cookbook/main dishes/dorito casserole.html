Dorito Casserole

Amethystmoon

  • 2 bags of Doritos
  • 2 lbs of Hamburger meat
  • 2 lg. cans of refried beans
  • 1 pkg. of taco seasoning
  • 1 lb of grated cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Sour Cream
  • Hot Sauce
  • 9 X 13" cake pan

Preheat the oven to 350°F. Brown HB meat in a pan drain and add taco seasoning. Heat up beans in a sauce pan. Smash until flat one bag of Doritos in bottom of cake pan. Pour beans over the chips then pour meat over beans. Sprinkle cheese over meat and heat in oven until cheese is melted. While it's cooking in the oven chop up veggies. When the cheese is melted it's ready to eat. Just slice and put on plate then add what ever veggies you wish to it.

Second bag of Doritos is for eating along with it.
