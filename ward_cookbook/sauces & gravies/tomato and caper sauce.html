Tomato And Caper Sauce

Chi Kali, Arizona

  • 2 - 6 oz cans tomato paste
  • 1 c. beef broth
  • 1 tsp. caper juice
  • 1 T. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. white pepper
  • dash of cayenne pepper
  • 3 T. capers

Combine all ingredients except the capers in a saucepan. Bring to a boil over moderate heat stirring constantly. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in capers.
