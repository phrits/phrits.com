Bath Balls

Take 1/2 cup each:

  • dried mint
  • rosemary
  • lavender flowers
  • rose petals
  • comfrey leaves
  • lovage

Blend well. Cut 4-5" squares of fabric, and place 1-2 teaspoons of herbs into each square. Pull corners together and tie with ribbon or yarn. Use one herbal ball per bath.
