Sunburn Spray

Candace

  • 1 c. warm water
  • 25 drops lavender essential oil
  • 25 drops chamomile essential oil
  • 12 drops eucalyptus essential oil

Mix together in a small spray bottle, preferably a glass one, and shake vigorously before each application. Spray affected area several times per day (after doing the required patch test, of course!)
