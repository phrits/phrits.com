FABULOUS FACIAL STEAM

Candace

  • 1/2 c. Dried Peppermint
  • 1/3 c. Dried Rose Petals
  • 1/2 c. Dried Chamomile Flowers
  • 1/2 c. Dried Comfrey
  • 1/2 c. Dried Lavender

Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.

Place 1/4 to 1/2 cup herbs into One Cup Boiling Water. Remove Water from Heat and lean face into steam. Cover top of your head with a towel, forming a tent over the steam. Stay under for at least one minute - but the longer you remain the better the benefits.

Save the extra herbs in an Airtight jar. An Apothecary Jar makes a nice addition to most bathroom counter tops and looks beautiful too..

All of the above items may be purchased at most Heath Food Stores.
