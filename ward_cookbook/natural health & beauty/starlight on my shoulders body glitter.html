Starlight On My Shoulders Body Glitter

Candace

  • 1/2 c. Aloe Vera Gel
  • 1 T. Vegetable Glycerin
  • 1 capsule Vitamin E
  • 1 1/2 tsp. Glitter or shimmer dust

Mix all ingredients together in a glass measuring cup. Store in an airtight container to avoid evaporation. Glass works better than plastic - canning jars work great.

The above items may be purchased at Drug Stores or Wal-Mart type stores.

The Glitter or shimmer may be purchased at a Hobby or Craft Store.
