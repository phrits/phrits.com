100% NATURAL LIP CARE BALM


Candace

  • 1 1/4 oz. Castor oil
  • 1/4 oz. Jojoba Oil
  • 1/4 oz. Shea Butter
  • 1/2 oz. Beeswax
  • 1 Vitamin E capsules
  • 22 drops Flavor oil

Microwave Directions:

Measure ingredients using kitchen scale.

Melt together Beeswax, Castor Oil, Jojoba Oil, and Shea Butter in glass measuring cup by placing in the microwave on low for 1 minute. Stir gently. If not melted - return to microwave at 30 second intervals.

Remove from Microwave and let cool for a minute or two.

Add Flavor oil and Vitamin E.

Pour liquid balm into small jars or tubes. After one hour, put on the lids.

Makes approximately six small 1/2 oz. jars.

Stove-top Directions:

Measure ingredients using kitchen scale.

Melt together Beeswax, Castor Oil, Jojoba Oil and Shea Butter, over very low heat. (Do not use aluminum pan.) Stir gently. When all ingredients are melted, remove from heat and let cool for a minute or two.

Add Flavor oil and Vitamin E. Pour into glass measuring cup (easier to handle than pan). Pour liquid balm into small jars or tubes and let cool.

After one hour, put on the lids.

Makes approximately six small jars.
