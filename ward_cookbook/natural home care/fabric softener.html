Fabric Softener

Kimberly Beaupre

  • 2 cups water
  • 3/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1 cup baking soda
  • 15 drops lavender essential oil

add 3/4–1 cup of this to rinse cycle. Will soften and deodorize clothes!

For variation add lemon, peppermint or pine essential oil in place of the lavender.
