Cleanser

- Kimberly Beaupre

  • 1 cup borax
  • 2 cups baking soda
  • 2 gallons water
  • 30 drops lavender
  • 20 drops pine
  • 10 drops thyme

or add this blend of oils to the borax mixture:

  • 20 drops of
  • grapefruit
  • lemon and basil oils

Use for counters, floors walls etc. Store in dark glass to protect oils.
