Irish Creme Liquor

Chi-kali, Arizona

  • 1 3/4 c. Irish whiskey
  • 2-3 T. boiling water
  • 1 T. instant coffee
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2 T. chocolate syrup
  • 1 T. vanilla

Blend well and refrigerate. Be aware that the fat and the liquor will want to separate, so before serving, you might want to run it all through the blender again. If a little thinner creme is desired, add a half pint of half-n-half or whole cream.
