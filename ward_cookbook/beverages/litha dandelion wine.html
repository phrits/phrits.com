Litha Dandelion Wine

Chi-Kali, Arizona

  • 1 qt. dandelion blossoms
  • 1 gal. hot water
  • 3 1/2 lb. sugar
  • 3 lemons, cut up
  • 2 oranges, cut up

Pour hot water over dandelion blossoms. Let stand for 24 hours.

Strain in a jelly bag. Heat juice again and add sugar, lemons and oranges.

Reheat, then put in a stone jar. Allow to ferment and skim the top everyday for 6 or 7 weeks, then bottle.
